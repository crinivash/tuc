Our Mission and Vision

Thongjao Union Club was established with a profound commitment to fostering holistic development within our cherished village. We firmly believe in the enduring power of community and the unifying spirit of sports, particularly football, to serve as catalysts for profound and positive change.

Our Objectives for Village Development

  • To initiate and actively support projects aimed at enhancing vital infrastructure, ensuring improved sanitation, and broadening access to essential basic amenities for all residents.
  • To vigorously promote education, cultivate digital literacy, and create accessible skill development opportunities for all age groups, with a special emphasis on empowering our youth for future success.
  • To meticulously preserve and vibrantly celebrate the rich cultural heritage, time-honored traditions, and unique local crafts that define the soul of Thongjao.
  • To actively encourage and facilitate broad community participation and collaborative decision-making processes, ensuring that development initiatives truly reflect the needs and aspirations of our villagers.
  • To diligently advocate for and streamline access to beneficial government schemes and resources specifically designed to accelerate rural development and uplift livelihoods.

Our Objectives for Football

  • To provide a structured, supportive, and inspiring platform where local talent can not only showcase but also rigorously develop their football skills to their fullest potential.
  • To passionately promote a healthy, active, and disciplined lifestyle among the youth through the compelling medium of sports, instilling values that extend beyond the field.
  • To meticulously build and maintain a competitive yet spirited football team that proudly represents Thongjao in various local and regional tournaments, instilling village pride with every match.
  • To actively foster and reinforce the critical values of sportsmanship, effective teamwork, strong leadership, and unwavering discipline among our dedicated players and wider community members.
  • To regularly organize engaging friendly matches, exciting tournaments, and community-wide football events that unite the entire village in celebration of the sport and a sense of shared camaraderie.

Through our integrated and dedicated efforts in both village development and the promotion of football, we are relentlessly striving to forge a vibrant, progressive, and truly thriving community in Thongjao, empowering its residents for a brighter and more prosperous future.
