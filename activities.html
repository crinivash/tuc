Our Community Activities

Thongjao Union Club is committed to enhancing the quality of life in our village through diverse community initiatives. From cleanliness drives to educational workshops, our activities foster unity and progress.

Our Initiatives

  • Village Cleanliness Drives: Regular campaigns to keep Thongjao clean and green, involving hundreds of volunteers.
  • Educational Workshops: Free programs on digital literacy, vocational skills, and academic support for youth.
  • Tree Plantation Campaigns: Planting trees to promote environmental sustainability in and around Thongjao.
  • Health Camps: Organizing free medical check-ups and awareness sessions for the community.
