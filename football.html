Thongjao FC: Our Pride

Thongjao FC represents the heart and spirit of our village, competing in local and regional tournaments with passion and sportsmanship. Our team is dedicated to nurturing young talent and fostering community pride through football.

Team Overview

Founded: 2015

Coach: Leimapokpam Rohit Singh

Home Ground: Thongjao Village Playfield

Thongjao FC has won several accolades, including the Tripura District Championship in 2020. Our players train rigorously to uphold the village's honor in every match.

Recent Achievements

  • Champions, Tripura District Championship 2020
  • Runners-Up, Agartala League 2022
  • Best Team Spirit Award, Regional Tournament 2023
